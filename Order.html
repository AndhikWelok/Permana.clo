How To Order

To order permana.clo, the method is:

  1. press the order button on the web
  2. will then be redirected to the chat page with permana.clo
  3. then you just order according to what you want
  4. for custom, you can directly share the design you made or request designs and clothes that can be made directly by permana.clo
  5. payment can be contacted on the chat page
  6. after payment the order will be processed immediately
